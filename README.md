# 🚀 ARC AI Finance

AI-powered personal and business finance automation platform built with **Telegram Bot, Groq LLM, n8n, and Google Sheets**.

ARC AI Finance helps users record expenses and income using natural conversations, voice messages, or receipt photos. Every transaction is automatically processed by AI and organized into an interactive financial dashboard.

# ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Finance Assistant

- Natural language expense tracking
- Income tracking
- AI-powered transaction recognition
- Powered by Groq Llama 3.3 70B

---

### 📸 Receipt OCR

Upload a receipt photo and the AI automatically extracts:

- Amount
- Category
- Description
- Date

and saves everything into Google Sheets.

---

### 🎤 Voice Recording

Users can simply send a voice message such as:

> "I spent Rp50.000 for lunch."

The AI converts speech into structured financial records automatically.

---

### 📊 Smart Financial Dashboard

Automatically generates:

- Daily Reports
- Weekly Reports
- Monthly Reports
- Income Reports
- Expense Reports
- Financial Summary

inside Google Sheets.

---

### ⚡ Workflow Automation

Every transaction is processed automatically using n8n.

Workflow includes:

Telegram

↓

Groq AI

↓

Transaction Classification

↓

Google Sheets

↓

Dashboard Update
